About Us

Best Customer Experience

Car.Hub offers unlimited free mileage as a standard, and if you’re hitting the open road with another person, Alamo outlines a number of circumstances for which it will waive the additional driver fee. These include if the second rental car driver is the spouse or domestic partner of the registered driver, or if the additional driver is a business partner or employer when driving on a corporate contract. If you’re a Car.Hub member, you won’t be charged an additional driver fee either. Other benefits include the Alamo Insider program, which gives customers a guaranteed 5 percent discount. Rotating discounts include deals for army members and free car upgrades for certain categories of drivers.
